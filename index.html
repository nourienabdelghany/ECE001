Artificial Intelligence

What's Artificial Intelligence? and Why Is Artificial Intelligence Important?

The term artificial intelligence (AI),was coined by John McCarthy in 1956. It means
the capability of a machine to imitate intelligent human behavior, or in another way
it's the ability of a computer program or a machine to think and learn.Today,
artificial intelligence is taking root in our everyday lives from morning until night.
